Welcome to Crazy Castles



Crazy Castles is a reservation app that allows users to play a game, earn a discount and book a stay at one of our enchanted castles.
Offering a wide variety of enchantments we ensure fun for all ages.
To take a look at the pictures of them, just click the next and previous buttons in the slideshow carousel.
If you want to play the game, click the Play button.
If you want to see the castles and/or book, click the see castles and book button.
