Available castles



This is the list of our available castles. On the left, choose a castle in the list, or search for your desired one with the filter button.
A new dialog will appear letting you select what enchantments are indispensable for you:

  • Apparitions of ghosts
  • Drop in temperature
  • Lights blinking
  • Moving objects
  • Nauseous smells
  • Strange noises

You can also select the maximum price you are willing to pay per night by typing it in the text fiel at the bottom.
Finally, click Apply to apply the filters. You will see those castles that fit them in the list on the left.

On the right you will see important information about the castles together with a picture.
Clicking select will select the castle to proceed with the reservation process.
