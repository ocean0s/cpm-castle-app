Reservation process



The process of booking a stay at a castle requires of some personal information:

  • Date of arrival
  • Number of nights
  • Number of people
  • Number of rooms
  • Name and surname
  • ID
  • Email
  • Comments (optional)

An important note is that a minimum of 1 person and a maximum of 2 people can stay at the same room.
Price is based on the number of rooms and nights, not per person.
Input your ID and press "Apply discount" if you have a discount code to apply the percentage off. The difference in saving will be reflected between the total and subtotal fields at the bottom left of the window.
To check that you have selected your desired castle, a brief section of information about the castle will appear on the left of the window, too.
Click "Go back" if you have changed your mind about the castle to select a different one.
Otherwise, click continue to proceed to the summary window.


The summary will show all the information you gave after checking that the inputs are valid.
Click "Go back" if there is an error. Otherwise, click "Continue" to formalize and write your reservation.


If you see this window, the reservation process was successful.
Thank you for your reservation and have a great stay!
