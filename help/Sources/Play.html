Play the game


The game consists of ghostbusters hunting ghosts.
These ghosts belong to 5 different squads represented by a different graphic. There is also a leader ghost at the top of the board.
The objective is to kill at least one ghost of each squad, and try to eliminate the leader ghost to. In order to do that, the ghostbuster will move vertically up in the board the number of cells dictated by the throw of a die (1 or 2 steps).
Meeting the first goal requirement results in getting an EXTRA10 discount coupon for a 10% off.
Meeting both requirements results in an EXTRA25 coupon for a 25% off in your reservation.
You can play again until you get the discount you want.
After playing you will be prompted to save the discount if you obtained one, and associate it to you DNI.
